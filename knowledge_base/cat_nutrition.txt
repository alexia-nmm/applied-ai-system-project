Cats are obligate carnivores and require animal protein to survive. They cannot thrive on a vegetarian diet.
Cats should never eat onions, garlic, chocolate, caffeine, alcohol, grapes, raisins, or xylitol.
Adult cats should be fed twice a day. Free feeding dry food can lead to obesity.
Cats need a diet high in moisture. Wet food helps prevent urinary tract issues and kidney disease.
Taurine is an essential amino acid for cats. A deficiency causes heart disease and blindness. Most commercial cat foods include it.
