Dogs should not eat grapes, raisins, chocolate, onions, garlic, xylitol, macadamia nuts, or avocado. These are toxic and can cause kidney failure, seizures, or death.
Adult dogs generally need to be fed twice a day. Puppies under 6 months need three meals a day.
Fresh water should always be available. Dehydration in dogs can cause lethargy, dry gums, and sunken eyes.
Protein should be the first ingredient in a dog's food. Look for chicken, beef, or fish as the primary source.
Overweight dogs are at higher risk for joint problems, diabetes, and heart disease. Portion control and regular exercise are key.
