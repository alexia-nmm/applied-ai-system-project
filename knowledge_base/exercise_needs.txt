High-energy dog breeds like Border Collies, Huskies, and Labrador Retrievers need at least 1-2 hours of exercise daily.
Small breeds like Chihuahuas and Shih Tzus need around 30 minutes of exercise per day.
Senior dogs should still exercise daily but with shorter, gentler walks to protect aging joints.
Cats need 10-15 minutes of active play twice a day to maintain healthy weight and mental stimulation.
Lack of exercise in dogs can lead to destructive behavior, anxiety, and obesity.
Interactive toys, laser pointers, and feather wands are good ways to exercise indoor cats.
