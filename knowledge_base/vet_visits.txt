Puppies need a series of vaccines starting at 6-8 weeks: distemper, parvovirus, and adenovirus. Rabies vaccine is given at 12-16 weeks.
Adult dogs need annual wellness exams. Core vaccines like rabies and DHPP are typically boostered every 1-3 years.
Kittens need vaccines starting at 6-8 weeks: FVRCP (feline viral rhinotracheitis, calicivirus, panleukopenia). Rabies at 12-16 weeks.
Adult cats need annual wellness exams. Indoor cats still need core vaccines and parasite prevention.
Senior pets (dogs over 7, cats over 10) should visit the vet every 6 months. Age-related conditions like arthritis, kidney disease, and dental disease are common.
Dental cleanings under anesthesia are recommended every 1-2 years for most pets to prevent periodontal disease.
